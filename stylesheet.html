Salmon Cookies Style Guide

Color Palette

Since we are mixing cookies with salmon, we want to stick to a combination of browns (cookies) and coral or salmon coloring.

The following can be considered OK colors to use on the site:

  • Hex: #7A4513

    RGB: 122, 69, 19

  • Hex: #A97038

    RGB: 169, 112, 56

    RGB: 214, 159, 107

    RGB: 255, 210, 167

    RGB: 255, 229, 204
